STUDY PLANNER WEB APP

Project Overview

The main goal of this project was to play around with Agentic AI tools to create a web-based application that helps students organize their study schedule and track progress, bringing in aspects of data analytics. The app allows users to input their courses, assignments, and study goals, and then generates a personalized study plan based on their preferences and deadlines. The design of the app is clean and user-friendly, with a focus on functionality and ease of use.

The structure includes a 'Planner' page, which allows users to add courses and course grades, color coding them for when course tasks appear on the calendar. The calendar may toggle between a monthly and weekly view depending on user preferences. Users may add coursework which will appear on the calendar on the respective date - the coursework may be categorized as a Test, Quiz, or Assignment. Separate fields have also been added to view current courses and upcoming coursework.

Another key feature is the study timer, which allows users to track their study sessions and manage their time more effectively. The 'History' page allow users to view sesssion history and performance metrics, including days in which they logged the most time studying for a specific course, and the most studied course.
